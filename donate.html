Volunteer & Donate

Your generosity empowers us to make a tangible difference. Every contribution, big or small, helps us build a safer and more inclusive future for LGBTQ+ individuals across Africa.

Your generous contribution directly fuels our mission to create a safer, more inclusive environment for LGBTQ+ individuals across Africa. Specifically, your donation helps us provide:

  • Emergency Aid: Secure housing, food, and medical assistance for those facing immediate threats or displacement due to their sexual orientation or gender identity.
  • Advocacy and Awareness: Funding initiatives that educate communities, challenge discriminatory laws, and promote human rights through peaceful means.
  • Support Networks: Establishing and strengthening safe spaces and confidential support groups for LGBTQ+ Africans to connect, share experiences, and receive counseling.
  • Legal Assistance: Connecting individuals with legal aid to navigate unjust situations and fight for their fundamental rights.

Thank you for partnering with us to uplift and protect vulnerable communities.
