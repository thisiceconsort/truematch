Who We Are

Milaaje is a non-governmental organization (NGO) dedicated to protecting the rights and ensuring the safety of lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, intersex, and asexual individuals and their allies across the African continent. We specialize in providing emergency **transfer and relocation services** for those facing immediate threat, coupled with long-term advocacy for policy change and community empowerment. We are activists and caregivers, ensuring a safer future.

Our Essential Services

  • Emergency Transfer

    Urgent and confidential relocation services for individuals in immediate danger across borders or to safer regions.

  • Safe Housing Network

    Connecting those displaced with vetted safe houses and temporary accommodation options.

  • Legal & Policy Advocacy

    Fighting for the decriminalization and protection of LGBTQIA+ rights at local and international levels.

  • Activist Training

    Empowering community members with the skills and resources needed for safe and effective activism.

  • Psychosocial Support

    Providing access to trauma-informed counseling and mental health resources for recovery.

  • Resource Mobilization

    Connecting individuals with vital resources, including employment, education, and medical aid.
